About Us

“Pillars and Mission Statement” Artistry - education through storytelling Creativity - resourcefulness and determination Governance - transparency and responsibility Operations - honesty and integrity "Artistry, Creativity, Operations, Governance"

"The mission of NDC is to operate a professional dance company devoted to the performance, production and preservation of dance styles and techniques reflective of the African American dance heritage and experience; offer and provide high quality arts programming; and to make art accessible to audiences of all ages and cultural backgrounds"

“Core Values” "Inspiration - redefining what it means to be human by telling the untold stories of cultures around the world Community - promoting the unity of diverse communities in Chicago and around the world by generating awareness of African American heritage and its instrumental role in the epic of human history
